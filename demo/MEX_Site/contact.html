Contact the Exchange

As the information hub of the Delaware River and Bay, the Maritime Exchange takes advantage of an enormous network of resources across the tri-state region and beyond. Through a strong membership of almost 300 businesses, a dedicated and active Board of Directors, and a knowledgeable and professional team of officers and staff, we are able to provide the services and information that helps make the Delaware River the premier port of call on the East Coast. Our team is a talented group of individuals who are dedicated to ensuring the success of our port region. We would be happy to answer any of your questions or concerns.
