Maritime On-Line

The Maritime Exchange serves as the electronic information center for Delaware River and Bay ship and cargo processing. The Exchange provides comprehensive on-line services to fulfill a variety of commercial and security-related maritime needs. From current, advance, and historical ship schedules and reports, to the Customs and Border Protection (CBP) cargo clearance system status and the U.S. Coast Guard electronic Notice of Arrival/Departure (eNOA/D) submissions, Maritime On-Line® (MOL) is the one-stop information source for Delaware River and Bay activity. The Exchange TRACS and NOA/D systems are also available for use at ports throughout the U.S.
